Event Details

First Round Second Round Final Round

First Round

1. Participants (Student/Corporate) are allowed to participate in a team of two or three.

2. The 1st round will be an elimination round and it will be in the form of a marketing quiz.

3. Only one of the team member will be allowed to sit for the first round (Marketing Quiz).

6. Top 10 teams will qualify for the next round on the basis of their score.

Second Round

1. For the 2nd round is the bidding round, in which 10 products will be dispayed by the volunteers and the teams have to bid the product.

2. The qualification criteria of the bidding will be based on the price margin and creativity with which the participants are able to sell it.

3. There will be a minimum bidding price (base price) for a particular product, and participants have to bid above that price and the ones who bids the highest will have to sell that product with that bidding price being the base price.

Final Round

1.The 3rd round is the selling round , in which each team will be given their bidding products , which they have to sell in the UNIVERSITY area.
2. And they have to sell the product within a time span of 45 minutes.
3. Team that will sell their product below bidding price will be disqualified.
4. Winners will be decided based on the price margin between the base price and the price in which the product is sold in the least span of time.
