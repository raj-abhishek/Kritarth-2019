Rules & Regulation


1. Participation: Solo, Duet, Trio and Teams (no size limit for teams).
2. Time Limit :Minimum: 90 Sec & Maximum:7+2 mins
3. It is a 2 day event
4. The event has only one round which should be considered as the final round and judging will be based on the act displayed on this round.
5. You can showcase any talent that you have decided to perform but within the given time limit.
6. Use of fire, water and any obscene object or portraying indecency in any form will straight away lead to disqualification.

Judging Criteria

1. Judging Criteria: Creativity, Technique, Stage Presence, connection with the audience and portrayal of theme, Props usage.
2. A person is bound to stop the performance midway if directed by the judge.
3. The judge’s decision is final and no acquisitions or queries shall be entertained.
4. Props and instruments are to be brought by the performers.
5. Time will start the moment participant enters the stage and stops when the participant leaves.
6. Music should be brought in pendrives.

Stukkad Rules

1. Time limit is 20 mins.
2. A team of maximum 20 members including 2 instrumentalists is allowed.
3. The theme for stage play should reveal the social issue, and should clearly illustrate the message as well.
4. Teams are free to use props in their act of performance.
5. Decision of judges will be final and binding.
