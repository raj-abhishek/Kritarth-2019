Event Details

First Round Final Round

First Round

1. Each student takes part in event as an individual participant. The event is divided into 2 rounds. After round 1 the names of the qualifying participants will be announced and they will then move to the finals.

2. The first round is an extempore round where each participant gets a minute to prepare and a minute to advocate unpopular views in support of a particular and publicly disputed topic.

Final Round

1. The finale will be an Oxford style extempore cum Q&A round, where selected participants get 5 minutes to prepare and 3 minutes to advocate a given topic, followed by a 2 minute Q&A session involving the other finalists.

2. Winners will be decided on the parameters of innovation, convincing skills and overall impact.
