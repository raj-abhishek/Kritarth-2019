Kanvassing

A pencil, a brush & few colors or sometimes may be just one of these is enough to create a piece of art that can make people stop and wonder. Our aim this time is to stop not just at wondering but create such pieces that make people wonder and reflect on themselves as well as their near and dear ones and the lifestyle they are leading. India against Negativity and Art of Giving are the themes decided.
