Rules & Regulation

The Contest is open to any participant either a student or a corporate, and also a business not more than one year at the time of entry. In order to be considered an eligible idea for purposes of this Contest, the business must meet all of the following criteria:

1. Participants must be atleast 18 years of age at the time of registration.

2. Participants need to provide their detailed profile.

3. Participants need to tell about their idea, concerned area and services.

4. Participants need to prepare a presentation on their idea/work, a detailed write-up and a prototype/ product (if any).

Other Details

1. Applicants will be shortlisted on the basis of their submitted profiles.

2. Shortlisted participant would be judged on the basis of ideation, inspiration, methodology, analysis, contribution to society and problem solving mechanism (if any).

3. Judges decision will be final and no acquisition will be entertained.
